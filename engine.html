Engine Removal

  1. Raise seat and disconnect battery terminals.
  2. Safely raise and support rear of unit so rear wheels are off the ground.
  3. Remove PTO belt tensioner spring (A) from the deck to relieve PTO belt tension (Fig. 050). See PTO Belt Replacement 48, 54 & 60 Inch Deck on page 6-5.
  4. Remove PTO belt fro PTO clutch.
  5. Disconnect the wire harness connection from the PTO clutch.
  6. Remove the PTO clutch bolt and then remove the clutch from the engine crankshaft.
  7. Remove the drive belt tensioner spring (B).
  8. Remove the belt from around the crankshaftdrive pulley and remove the drive pulley from the engine crankshaft. Make note of pulley orientation for assembly.
  9. If necessary, remove rear engine guard.
  10. Remove the fasteners that secure the heat shield to the muffler and frame and remove the heat shield. If necessary, remove the muffler from the engine.
  11. Remove the (4) engine to frame mounting bolts. Make note of clutch stop bracket (C) mounting and orientation.
  12. Safely disconnect the fuel and vent lines from engine.
  13. Remove the throttle and hoke cable from carburetor linkage.
  14. Disconnect the chassis-to-engine electrical connection.
  15. Disconnect the positive battery cable (F) from the starter motor (Fig. 052).
  16. The engine can now be lifted vertically from the frame.

Engine Installation

  1. Position the engine onto the frame.
  2. Connect the battery ground wires (E) to the engine block, and connect the positive battery wire (F) to the engine starter motor.
  3. Connect the chassis to engine harness connector (D).
  4. Install the fuel and vent lines to the engine.
  5. Install the throttle and choke cables to carburetor linkage.
  6. Apply Loctite (R) 242 to the (4) engine mounting bolts.
  7. By hand, install the (4) engine mounting bolts.

    8. Note: Some of the engine mounting bolts also secure the clutch stop bracket and stiffener tube to the frame. See the exploded views for application and mounting location.

  8. In a cross pattern, torque the 94) engine mounting bolts to specification - 33 ft-lbs. (44 Nm).
  9. Install heat shield to the muffler mounting screws and torque to specification - 5 ft-lbs. (7 Nm)
  10. Install the heat shield and muffler to frame mounting screws and torque to specification - 17 ft-lbs. (23 Nm).
  11. Apply anti-sieze to the engine crakshaft and the ends of the drive pulley onto the crakshaft and hold in position.

    12. Note: The drive pulley is marked "CLUTCH SIDE" for proper installation (Fig. 054).

  12. Install the drive belt around the drive pulley.
  13. Install the drive belt tensioner spring as oriented below (Fig. 055).
  14. Aplly Loctite (R) 242 to the PTO clutch bolt threads.
  15. Install the PTO clutch onto the crankshaft. Be sure PTO clutch engages with the clutch stop bracket.
  16. Install and torque the PTO clutch bolt to specification - 55 ft-lbs. (76 Nm).
  17. Install PTO belt onto PTO clutch. Verify proper PTO belt routing; see "PTO" Belt Replacement amd Belt Routing" on page 6-4. Install the PTO belt tensioner spring to the deck.
  18. Connect battery cables and verify engine oil level.
  19. Safely start engine and verify proper operation.
